Who we are

AgentsOz is a moniker for the Agent Group within the School of Computer Science and Information Technology at RMIT University. The research group has three core research areas: agent reasoning (e.g., goal reasoning, plan coordination, failure recovery, goal-plan conflict/resolution), agent-oriented software engineering (e.g., design, testing, software methodologies & tools, automatic code generation) and agent-based simulation (including serious games and interactive simulation, application areas focussing on climate change adaptation and environmental issues.)

This is the team page listing the various open source repositories managed by the group, that are hosted on GutHub.
